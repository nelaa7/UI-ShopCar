A6 Saloon

Progress is not just felt.
It is experienced.
The new A6 Saloon.

When design meets efficiency and sportiness meets style. When innovation and precision go hand-in-hand and progressive elegance becomes palpable in every line. When form and function become a statement and progress stands above all else. That's when executive class has a new pace setter: the Audi A6 Saloon. More athletic than ever. As supreme as always.

  • The wide, deep-positioned single-frame grill, the flat headlights and the powerfully contoured air intakes radiate a sporty look.

Clear statement.
From every perspective.
From the widened single frame, to the side air intakes with sporty chrome trims, to the narrow headlights – the progressive nature A6 Saloon's front gives a great impression at first sight. The sharpened shoulder line with strong curves above the wheel arches accentuates the quattro inheritance. The exterior mirrors have a sporty location by the armrest. Moreover, the coupé-like flowing roofline elegantly leads into the rear end, formed in three dimensions with a characteristic aluminium style.

Groundbreaking brilliance.
Expressive look.

The optional HD Matrix LED headlights with dynamic indicator light impress from two different perspectives. They provide the driver with optimum road lighting without dazzling oncoming traffic, and maximise the A6 Saloon's effective beam width thanks to the outward focus of the characteristic lighting segments.

More about the lighting technology

Mood enhancer

The optional contour ambient light package creates a unique atmosphere and gives the interior of the A6 Saloon a fascinating ambience, characterised by a sense of well-being. Precise contour light guides accentuate the instrument panel, central console and other striking design structures in 30 different colour tones with continuous dimming.

Reduced design.
Maximum ease of operation.

The high-resolution 10.1-inch and 8.6-inch HD MMI High touch displays with touch response, available on request, fit seamlessly into the elegant instrument panel and are optimally tailored to the needs of the driver. On the upper display, the driver manages information, entetainment and navigation services; on the lower display, text input, air conditioning and various comfort functions can be controlled.

More about the operating concept

Intelligence, intelligently deployed

The future now: A combination of perfectly tuned sensors, cameras, radar and ultrasound technology detects and assesses your driving situation, assists you when you need it and intervenes if there is no alternative. Join us on the way to an autonomous driving experience so that you can drive with it tomorrow.

Performer

For its European brand launch, the A6 Saloon starts with two powerful engines – a diesel and a petrol engine. The petrol engine is a 3.0 TFSI1. With 340 hp and 500 Nm of torque, the six-cylinder engine accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in 5.1 seconds. Similar performance is offered by the 3.0 TDI2 V6 diesel engine with 286 hp and 620 Nm torque. All engines are equipped with the new mild hybrid technology.

More about the engine

More agile. More manoeuvrable. Sportier.

The standard progressive steering system ensures powerful driving dynamics through an emphatically sporty transmission, which, especially in combination with the optional dynamic all-wheel steering system, increases agility and manoeuvrability. The optionally available 21″ wheels underline the athleticism and at the same time offer improved noise comfort.

For eight generations.

Creating innovations with vision. Turning digitisation into reality. With a forward-looking operating concept, extensive networking and a wide range of driver assistance systems. For more safety. Enhanced comfort. And for new standards in the luxury class. That is our aspiration. That is our passion. That is the eighth-generation A6 Saloon.
